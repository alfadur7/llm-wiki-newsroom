## Overview

DeepSeek is the AI company whose R1 model, released in January 2025 under the permissive MIT license, is a prominent [[OpenWeights]] example. DeepSeek published R1's weights and parameters along with a technical paper describing its Mixture-of-Experts architecture, but not the underlying training data — which is why R1 is classified as open weights rather than fully [[OpenSourceAI]]. R1 caused significant disruption on claims that it matched well-known LLMs while costing roughly $6 million to train, a fraction of comparable models. Because the weights are MIT-licensed and public, users can [[FineTuning|fine-tune]] R1 on their own data.
